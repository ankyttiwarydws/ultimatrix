Artificial Intelligence (AI): The Next Frontier

While DRS and Hawk-Eye focus on decision-making and ball tracking, Artificial Intelligence is taking cricket analytics to the next level. AI-driven platforms process enormous datasets from matches, player statistics, and video feeds to uncover patterns, optimize strategies, and even predict outcomes.

AI tools help teams scout opponents, evaluate player performance, and devise tactics. They can automate match analysis, highlight strengths and weaknesses, and offer personalized training recommendations. For fans, AI-powered apps provide deeper insights, interactive experiences, and real-time stats during games.

AI is also being used to improve broadcasting with smarter camera angles, automated highlights, and enhanced commentary. Its continuous evolution promises an even more immersive cricket experience in the years to come.
