Amazon plans to borrow up to $42 billion in one of the biggest corporate bond sales ever

Amazon is reportedly planning a colossal bond sale, aiming to raise between $37 billion and $42 billion across 11 tranches. This massive borrowing, with maturities stretching to 2076, is primarily to fund its aggressive expansion in artificial intelligence infrastructure. This move significantly surpasses its previous $15 billion bond sale in late 2024.
