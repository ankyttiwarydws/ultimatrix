Russia will not disclose data on its crude export to India: Kremlin

Russia will not release crude oil export data to India, citing numerous "ill-wishers." This follows a US waiver allowing Indian refiners to purchase Russian oil amidst Middle East conflict, and reports of Russia ready to increase supplies to India and China.
