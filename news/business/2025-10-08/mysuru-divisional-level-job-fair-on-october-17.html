Mysuru divisional-level job fair on October 17

A mega divisional-level job fair will be held in the city on October 17 in which over 200 companies will participate. Mysuru Zilla Panchayat CEO S. Yukesh Kumar said the job fair will be held at the Maharaja’s College Grounds and urged youngsters to make good use of this opportunity.
