Kerala tourist buses suspend services to Karnataka, Tamil Nadu. Here's why

The Luxury Bus Owners Association in Kerala announced a suspension of all interstate tourist bus services to Karnataka and Tamil Nadu starting Monday at 6 pm due to heavy fines and unlawful taxes imposed by neighbouring states.
