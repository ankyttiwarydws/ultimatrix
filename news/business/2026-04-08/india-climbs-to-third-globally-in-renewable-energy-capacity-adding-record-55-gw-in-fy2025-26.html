India Climbs to Third Globally in Renewable Energy Capacity, Adding Record 55 GW in FY2025-26

This marks the highest annual capacity addition ever, surpassing the previous record of 29.5 GW achieved in FY2024‐25. India has risen to third place globally in renewable energy installed capacity, overtaking Brazil, according to Union Minister for New and Renewable Energy, Pralhad Venkatesh Joshi.
