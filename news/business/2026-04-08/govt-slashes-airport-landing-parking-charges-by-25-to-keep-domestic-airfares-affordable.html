Govt slashes airport landing & parking charges by 25% to keep domestic airfares affordable

Civil Aviation Minister Ram Mohan Naidu announces a 25% reduction in landing and parking charges for domestic airlines. This ₹400 crore relief measure aims to offset rising jet fuel costs and prevent a hike in airfares for passengers over the next three months.
