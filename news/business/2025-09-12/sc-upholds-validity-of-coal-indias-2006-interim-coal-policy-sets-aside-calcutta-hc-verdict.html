SC upholds validity of Coal India's 2006 interim coal policy, sets aside Calcutta HC verdict

The Supreme Court has validated Coal India Limited's 2006 interim coal policy, which imposed a 20% price increase on coal supplied to non-core sector industries. Overturning a Calcutta High Court decision, the apex court affirmed Coal India's authority to regulate prices and dismissed claims that the policy violated constitutional rights. The court also rejected requests for refunds from private firms.
