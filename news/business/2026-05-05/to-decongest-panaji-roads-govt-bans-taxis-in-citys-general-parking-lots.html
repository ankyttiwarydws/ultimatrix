To decongest Panaji roads, govt bans taxis in city’s general parking lots

Panaji has banned taxis from general parking lots to ease congestion, with tourist taxis restricted to designated areas. The North Goa collector issued the notification, aiming to reduce roadside parking and improve traffic flow. Taxis can only halt for three minutes for pick-up/drop-off, and double parking will be addressed.
