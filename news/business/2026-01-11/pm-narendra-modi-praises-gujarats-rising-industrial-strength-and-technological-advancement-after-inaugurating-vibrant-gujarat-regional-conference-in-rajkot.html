PM Narendra Modi Praises Gujarat's Rising Industrial Strength and Technological Advancement After Inaugurating Vibrant Gujarat Regional Conference in Rajkot

PM Narendra Modi inaugurated the five-day business exhibition "Vibrant Gujarat" and the Vibrant Gujarat Regional Conference today, January 11 in Rajkot. He also praised Gujarat's rising industrial strength and technological advancement. The Prime Minister showed keen interest in the 'Ocean of Opportunities' pavilion, designed to highlight the untapped potential of the Kutch-Saurashtra coastline.
