PhysicsWallah IPO: Anchor Round Garners Over Rs 1,500 Crore Mostly From Mutual Funds

Mutual funds secured more than half the allocation of PhysicsWallah Ltd. shares in the pre-IPO anchor round on Monday. The mainboard issue is set to open on Nov. 11. The company raised Rs 1,560 crore from anchor investors. It allotted 14,33,80,733 shares at Rs 109 apiece to 57 entities.
