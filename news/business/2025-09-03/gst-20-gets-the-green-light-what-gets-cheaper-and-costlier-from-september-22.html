GST 2.0 gets the green light; what gets cheaper and costlier from September 22?

The GST Council has approved changes to India's indirect tax structure. Many daily-use goods will become cheaper from September 22. Food items, consumer goods, and renewable energy devices will see reduced tax rates. However, sin goods and luxury items will remain under higher taxation. Coal will be taxed at a higher rate. Restaurants and lottery services face revised rules.
