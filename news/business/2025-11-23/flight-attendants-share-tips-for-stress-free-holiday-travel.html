Flight attendants share tips for stress-free holiday travel

Flight attendants offer expert advice for navigating holiday travel chaos. Key tips include booking flights well in advance, allowing ample connection time, and flying midweek. They also emphasize packing light, staying hydrated, and taking health precautions to ensure a smoother journey home.
