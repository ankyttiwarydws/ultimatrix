Wealthy raises Rs 130 crore led by Bertelsmann India Investments

Wealthy, a wealth-management startup, secured Rs 130 crore in Series B funding led by Bertelsmann India Investments. The AI-powered platform for mutual fund distributors will use the capital to enhance technology, expand geographically, and grow its network. The company aims to empower independent professionals by streamlining operations and offering comprehensive financial products.
