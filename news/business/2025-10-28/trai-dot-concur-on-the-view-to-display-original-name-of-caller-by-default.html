Trai, DoT concur on the view to display original name of caller by default

Caller name display will now be enabled by default for all phone users. This move aims to curb fraud and cybercrime. The service will be available on 4G and newer technologies. Devices sold after a six-month period will feature this. The Department of Telecom will finalize the implementation details.
