PFRDA in talks with platforms to enable seamless NPS enrolment for gig workers, says Chairperson Ramann

PFRDA Chairperson Ramann acknowledged that gig and platform workers have so far remained outside the pension net but said this will change as pension funds launch dedicated schemes. He said more than one fund is preparing products for this segment, given its size and growth potential.
