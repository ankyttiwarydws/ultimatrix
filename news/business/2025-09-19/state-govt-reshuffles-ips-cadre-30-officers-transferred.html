State govt reshuffles IPS cadre: 30 officers transferred

The Jharkhand government has executed a significant reshuffle within its IPS cadre, transferring 30 officers and assigning new roles. Anurag Gupta has been relieved of his duties as DG CID. Rakesh Ranjan is the new SSP of Ranchi, succeeding Chandan Kumar Sinha, now DIG ACB.
