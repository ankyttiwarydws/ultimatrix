Cable TV service provider Satlinks alleges price bias, Star denies; TDSAT steps in

The Telecom Disputes Settlement and Appellate Tribunal (TDSAT) is addressing a petition by Satlinks against Star India over alleged pricing bias and billing disputes. Satlinks claims inflated charges and discriminatory rates affecting its subscriber base. Star India denies these allegations, citing payment irregularities and uniform pricing.
