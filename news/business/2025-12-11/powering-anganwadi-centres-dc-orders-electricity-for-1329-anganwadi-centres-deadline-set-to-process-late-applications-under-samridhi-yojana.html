Powering anganwadi centres: DC orders electricity for 1,329 anganwadi centres; Deadline set to process late applications under Samridhi Yojana

In a decisive move towards enhancing early childhood care, Deputy Commissioner Ajay Nath Jha of Bokaro has called for the swift electrification of 1,329 anganwadi centers utilizing maintenance funds. He urged the electricity department to act promptly and emphasized the importance of daily monitoring for accountability.
