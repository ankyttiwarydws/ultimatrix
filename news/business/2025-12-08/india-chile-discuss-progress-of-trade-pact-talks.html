India, Chile Discuss Progress Of Trade Pact Talks

India and South American country Chile on Monday reviewed the progress of a proposed trade pact between the two countries, an official statement said on Monday. It was discussed between Claudia Sanhueza, Treasury Undersecretary in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Chile, and Commerce and Industry Minister Piyush Goyal in New Delhi.
