MP Assembly: Congress walks out over Singrauli coal mine compensation

MP Congress staged a walkout from the assembly over the Singrauli coal mines issue, alleging irregularities in compensation for displaced tribals. The Opposition demanded a Joint Parliamentary Committee (JPC) probe, accusing the BJP govt of favouring Adani.
