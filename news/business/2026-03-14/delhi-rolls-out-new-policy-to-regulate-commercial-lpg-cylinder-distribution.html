Delhi rolls out new policy to regulate commercial LPG cylinder distribution

The Delhi government rolled out a new policy to counter the shortage of commercial LPG cylinders. Under the new scheme, a priority-based system will be initiated so that hospitals, railways, and airports will receive the gas first.
