Union min urges developing Ranchi as millet capital of the country

Union Minister Sanjay Seth urged agricultural scientists and farmers to make Ranchi the nation's millet hub. He pledged defence ministry support for developing and distributing millet products in military canteens. The minister also called for nutritious food solutions for soldiers.
