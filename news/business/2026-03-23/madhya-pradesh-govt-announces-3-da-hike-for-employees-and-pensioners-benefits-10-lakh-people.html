Madhya Pradesh govt announces 3% DA hike for employees and pensioners, benefits 10 lakh people

The Madhya Pradesh cabinet on Monday approved a 3% DA hike for 7 lakh employees and 3 lakh pensioners, costing ₹2450 crore annually. Additional welfare measures include micro irrigation projects, training for OBC youth, and increased nutrition for underweight children.
