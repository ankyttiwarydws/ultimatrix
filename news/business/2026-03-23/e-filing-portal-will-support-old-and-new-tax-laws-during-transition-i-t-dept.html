E-Filing Portal Will Support Old And New Tax Laws During Transition: I-T Dept

The Income Tax Department said its e-filing portal will support both old and new tax laws during the transition. The new Income Tax Act 2025 comes into effect from April 1, 2026, while earlier assessments will continue under the old law.
