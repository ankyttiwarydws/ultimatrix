Rs700-cr Ara-Sasaram rail doubling project gains pace

The railways in Western Bihar are on the brink of a transformative upgrade! With a whopping Rs 700-crore investment into the Ara-Sasaram line doubling project, the gears are already turning. Intensive surveys and land acquisitions are taking place in Bhojpur and Rohtas districts, with a roadmap to complete this vital infrastructure within three years.
