Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation to launch 6th Miyawaki plantation drive to enhance urban green cover

Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation will start its sixth Miyawaki plantation drive on September 17. The location is the bamboo farm at Odisha University of Agriculture and Technology. The aim is to convert five acres into a green space. Fifteen thousand saplings will be planted. This initiative will improve the city's green cover. It will also promote a healthier environment.
