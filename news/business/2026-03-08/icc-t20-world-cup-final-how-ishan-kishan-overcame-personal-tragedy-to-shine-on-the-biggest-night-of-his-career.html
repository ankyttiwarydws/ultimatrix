ICC T20 World Cup final: How Ishan Kishan overcame personal tragedy to shine on the biggest night of his career

Despite a tragic car accident claiming two relatives, including his cousin sister and brother-in-law, Ishan Kishan played in the T20 World Cup final. He scored a crucial 54 runs and took three catches, dedicating his performance to the heavens. Kishan, though not his usual cheerful self, performed admirably, securing his first ICC title.
