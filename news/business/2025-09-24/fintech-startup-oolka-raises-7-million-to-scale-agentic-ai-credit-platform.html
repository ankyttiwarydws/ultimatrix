Fintech startup Oolka raises $7 million to scale agentic AI credit platform

Oolka, a Bengaluru-based fintech startup, is leveraging agentic AI to offer personalised credit solutions to users, aiming to improve financial health and expand access to affordable credit. The company has already processed over ₹100 crores in repayments and is approaching 2 million users.
