Crisil to acquire McKinsey PriceMetrix Co

Crisil, an S&P Global company, announced the acquisition of McKinsey’s PriceMetrix, a Toronto-based wealth management analytics firm serving US and Canadian clients. With a database covering USD 8 trillion in assets and 30 million accounts, PriceMetrix products will bolster Crisil’s benchmarking and advisory services.
