Transaction day for treasuries on March 28

Kohima, March 26 (MExN): The Finance Department has declared March 28, as a transaction day for all treasuries and sub-treasuries in Nagaland. According to a notification, the day, which is otherwise a non-transaction day, has been designated to facilitate clearance of government bills during the closing days of the financial year 2025–26. All concerned have been informed accordingly.
