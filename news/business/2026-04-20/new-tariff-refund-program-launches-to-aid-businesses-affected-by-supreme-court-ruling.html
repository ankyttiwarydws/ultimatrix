New Tariff Refund Program Launches to Aid Businesses Affected by Supreme Court Ruling

The Trump administration is introducing a new tariff refund program aimed at helping businesses that paid duties later ruled invalid by the Supreme Court. Following a significant ruling that invalidated a key part of Trump's tariff policy, the program will utilize the CAPE portal to streamline refund claims. Eligible businesses, including importers and customs brokers, can apply for refunds through this online platform. The initiative is expected to cover a massive $166 billion in tariffs, although experts caution that consumers may not see immediate price reductions.
