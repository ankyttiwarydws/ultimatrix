‘Harassment’ of Final Fantasy 14 developers forces Square Enix to shut down another Japanese content creator

Square Enix has taken legal action against another Japanese content creator for allegedly harassing Final Fantasy 14 staff. This follows a similar case in March, with the company reaching a settlement that resulted in content removal and account termination. Square Enix emphasizes its commitment to protecting employees from harmful behavior.
