Govt distributes Rs 838 cr to farmers on Akshaya Tritiya

On Akshaya Tritiya, the Odisha government disbursed Rs 838.48 crore to 41.68 lakh farmers under the CM Kisan Yojana, providing Rs 2,000 each. Chief Minister Mohan Charan Majhi also launched the Ama Bihana Ratha to improve seed access and highlighted the state's agricultural growth and procurement efforts.
