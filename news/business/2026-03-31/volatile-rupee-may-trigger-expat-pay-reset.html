Volatile rupee may trigger expat pay reset

Companies are rethinking expat pay in India due to rupee volatility. Employment contracts may include new clauses for currency risks. Employers might voluntarily cover shortfalls to retain senior talent. This shift aims to manage financial uncertainties for both parties. The focus is on adapting compensation strategies to a changing economic landscape.
