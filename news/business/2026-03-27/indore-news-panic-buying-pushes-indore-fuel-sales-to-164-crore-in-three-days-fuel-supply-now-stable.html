Indore News: Panic Buying Pushes Indore Fuel Sales To ₹164 Crore In Three Days; ; Fuel Supply Now Stable

Indore residents spent Rs 164 crore on petrol and diesel from March 24–26 amid panic buying, with 163 lakh litres sold—far above normal daily consumption of 10 lakh litres petrol and 15 lakh litres diesel. Prices ranged Rs 106–107 for petrol and Rs 92 for diesel. Officials clarified there was no shortage, and supply has now stabilized.
