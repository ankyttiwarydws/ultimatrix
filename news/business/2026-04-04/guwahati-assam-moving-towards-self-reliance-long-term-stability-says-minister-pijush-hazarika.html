Guwahati: 'Assam Moving Towards Self-Reliance & Long-Term Stability,' Says Minister Pijush Hazarika

Assam minister Pijush Hazarika said the state has created over 1.65 lakh jobs and aims to cross 2 lakh, while expanding education and skill programmes. He also outlined a long-term plan to tackle floods through infrastructure upgrades, monitoring systems, and major investments over the next five years.
