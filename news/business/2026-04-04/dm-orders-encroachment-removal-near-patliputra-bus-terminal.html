DM orders encroachment removal near Patliputra bus terminal

Patna's District Magistrate has ordered a significant upgrade for Patliputra Bus Terminal, focusing on passenger amenities and traffic flow. Key directives include clearing encroachments, removing abandoned vehicles, and acquiring more land for infrastructure. A multi-departmental team will tackle debris dumping, while unauthorized operators face penalties. Enhanced security and improved facilities are top priorities.
