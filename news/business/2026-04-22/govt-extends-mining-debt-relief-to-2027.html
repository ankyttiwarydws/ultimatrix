Govt extends mining debt relief to 2027

The Goa government has extended its mining debt relief scheme until March 2027, providing financial assistance to truck and barge owners affected by the halt in the iron ore mining industry. This is the last extension for the scheme, which aids defaulters in settling loans taken before 2012.
