Aditya Birla Group-led consortium acquires Royal Challengers Bengaluru for USD 1.78 billion

A consortium led by the Aditya Birla Group (ABG) on Tuesday acquired 100 per cent equity stake in IPL franchise Royal Challengers Bengaluru for a whopping USD 1.78 billion (approximately Rs 16,706 crore) from its current owner the United Spirits Limited.
