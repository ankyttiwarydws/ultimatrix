'Energy conservation is not just an option, but the most crucial need': President Murmu

Addressing the gathering on the occasion of National Energy Conservation Day in New Delhi, the President described energy conservation as the most environmentally friendly and reliable source of energy. She emphasised that saving energy does not merely mean reducing consumption, but using energy "wisely, responsibly, and efficiently."
