Mumbai Monsoon Prep: BMC May Use Own Staff For Mithi River Desilting As Contractors Show No Interest

Facing a lack of contractor interest in the Mithi River desilting tender, the BMC may deploy its own staff and machinery to begin work before April 1 to prevent monsoon flooding in vulnerable areas like Kurla and Chunabhatti.
