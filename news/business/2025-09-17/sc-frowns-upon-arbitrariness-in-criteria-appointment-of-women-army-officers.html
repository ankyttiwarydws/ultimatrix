SC frowns upon 'arbitrariness' in criteria appointment of women Army officers

The Supreme Court has raised concerns about the unequal evaluation criteria for Short Service Commission (SSC) women Army officers seeking permanent commission compared to their male counterparts. The court questioned the gender-based differences in assessing officers with the same training and postings, highlighting potential violations of Articles 14 and 15 of the Constitution.
