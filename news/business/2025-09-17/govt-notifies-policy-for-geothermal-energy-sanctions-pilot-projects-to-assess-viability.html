Govt notifies policy for geothermal energy, sanctions pilot projects to assess viability

The union ministry of new and renewable energy in India has announced a policy to promote geothermal energy, aiming for net zero carbon emissions by 2070. It includes pilot projects and encourages research, collaboration, and innovative practices in the geothermal sector.
