GBA proposes to increase permissible deviation limit in building constructions from 5% to 15%

The five city corporations of Bengaluru have proposed to increase the permissible deviation limit in building constructions from the existing 5% to 15%, by amending building bylaws. The corporations have also proposed to impose a penalty for the regularisation of the deviation within permissible limits as a percentage of guidance value of the plot, indicating it is also a revenue-mopping exercise. The Greater Bengaluru Authority (GBA) has termed it a “people-friendly decision”.
