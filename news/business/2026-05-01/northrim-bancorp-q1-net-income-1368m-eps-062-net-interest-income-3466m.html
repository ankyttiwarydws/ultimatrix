Northrim Bancorp Q1 Net Income $13.68M, EPS $0.62; Net Interest Income $34.66M

Northrim Bancorp reported increased net income and earnings per share for the first quarter of 2026. Net income rose to $13.68 million, with basic earnings per share reaching $0.62. The company saw a significant increase in net interest income, while also recording a provision for credit losses compared to a benefit in the prior year. Total assets, deposits, and loans also showed growth.
