SBI Mutual Fund picks up 4% stake in Urban Company via bulk deals

SBI Mutual Fund acquired a 4% stake in Urban Company, buying 5.7 crore shares through bulk deals. The investment followed the end of a six-month lock-in period for pre-IPO investors, making 94.09 crore shares eligible for trading, amid ongoing stock price pressure.
