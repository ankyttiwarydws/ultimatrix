Bhiwandi Civic Body Clears Crore-Rupee Proposals Without Formal Agenda, Corporators Cry Foul Over Procedural Lapses

BNMC approved financial proposals worth over ₹22 crore, including ₹2.92 crore for drain desilting and ₹19.52 crore for a waste transfer station, during a special meeting. Corporators objected, alleging the agenda was not formally presented. Concerns were raised over cost disparities and transparency, while officials assured strict monitoring and timely execution before monsoon.
