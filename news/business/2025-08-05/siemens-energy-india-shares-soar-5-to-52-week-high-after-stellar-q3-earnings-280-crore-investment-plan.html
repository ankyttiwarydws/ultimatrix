Siemens Energy India shares soar 5% to 52-week high after stellar Q3 earnings, ₹280 crore investment plan

Siemens Energy India shares surged over 5% to a 52-week high of ₹3,407.95, following strong Q3 earnings with an 80% YoY profit jump to ₹263 crore and a revenue increase of 20%. The company plans a ₹280 crore investment to expand manufacturing in Aurangabad.
