Sikkim govt hikes Dearness Allowance by 5 per cent for employees and pensioners

The Government of Sikkim has announced an increase in Dearness Allowance (DA) and Dearness Relief (DR) for state employees and pensioners, according to a circular issued by the Finance Department on March 18.
