Pinaki Bhattacharya exits VML after 16-year stint

Pinaki Bhattacharya has exited VML (formerly JWT), bringing to a close a 16-year journey with the network. He last served as chief strategy officer. Bhattacharya joined JWT in 2010 as senior vice president and national planning director, and went on to lead strategy across the agency’s evolution into Wunderman Thompson and later VML. In 2021, he was elevated to chief strategy officer, where he oversaw strategic planning and direction for the agency. Announcing his departure on LinkedIn, Bhattacharya described his tenure as a “fabulous run”, highlighting the role of colleagues and teams in shaping his journey. He credited the people he worked with over the years as a key reason for his long association with the organisation. Over the course of his career, Bhattacharya has held senior strategy roles at agencies including Saatchi & Saatchi and FCB Ulka. He has worked across a wide range of categories such as consumer goods, automobiles, apparel and technology, handling brands like PepsiCo, Hero Honda, Tropicana and Timex.
