Watch | Omnicom bets on Acxiom data, AI to drive growth in India, Asia Pacific post IPG deal

The Omnicom Group is betting on data and artificial intelligence to drive growth across India and Asia Pacific following its acquisition of Interpublic Group (IPG), with executives highlighting the strategic value of Acxiom.Tony Harradine, CEO of Omnicom Media Asia Pacific, said the deal is about “the transformation of two great businesses coming together and forging something more powerful,” with Acxiom emerging as “a tremendous data asset” underpinning future technology platforms. He added that the integration will enable more centralised capabilities while enhancing consultative, end-to-end client solutions.In India, CEO Kartik Sharma noted that Acxiom brings access to over 520 million privacy-safe identities, helping sharpen precision marketing. “It helps us build on our precision capabilities,” he said, adding that the deal also blends Omnicom’s global client base with IPG’s strong local portfolio.The first 100 days have focused on integration and reassurance, with Sharma saying the transition has been “busy” but well received by clients and teams. Harradine emphasised minimal disruption, noting that “there is no day-to-day impact for clients.”The integration comes amid a broader industry shift, as agencies reposition themselves around data, AI and connected ecosystems to stay competitive.
