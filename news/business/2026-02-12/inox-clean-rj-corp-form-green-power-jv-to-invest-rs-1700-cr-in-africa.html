Inox Clean, RJ Corp form Green Power JV, to invest Rs 1,700 cr in Africa

Inox Clean Energy and RJ Corp have partnered in a new joint venture. The companies will invest 1,700 crore in Africa for renewable energy generation. This initiative aims to develop utility-scale solar projects. The first phase targets 570 MW capacity. The goal is to reach 2.5 GW by FY29. This marks a significant global expansion for Inox Clean Energy.
