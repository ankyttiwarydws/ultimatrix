Temporary bus stand inaugurated in Berhampur

A temporary bus stand has been inaugurated near Duduma colony in Berhampur to facilitate the ongoing construction of an overbridge at Gate Bazar. Developed by BeDA on 4 acres, the temporary facility aims to divert buses and ease traffic congestion. The Rs 1.50 crore project includes a shed, toilets, and ticket counters, with plans for further expansion.
