STT hike won't curb speculation, will boost options: Nithin Kamath

Zerodha's Nithin Kamath says the STT hike won't reduce F&O speculation and will push more trading to options. He suggests product suitability norms as a better approach than repeated tax hikes, which risk making trading unviable.
