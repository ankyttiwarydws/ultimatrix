U.S. Launches 'Project Vault' for Strategic Mineral Stockpile

In a significant move to secure the United States' mineral supply, President Donald Trump announced 'Project Vault' on Monday, a strategic stockpile initiative backed by $10 billion in seed funding alongside $2 billion from private interests. The project is designed to ensure American businesses are protected from shortages of critical minerals.
