IndiGo flight cancellations: Airline cancels 57 flights due to 'bad weather' at various airports

IndiGo has cancelled 57 flights across its network due to bad weather. The airline also plans to cancel 13 more flights for Sunday, citing operational reasons and forecasted poor weather. This follows earlier cancellations due to pilot duty norms.
