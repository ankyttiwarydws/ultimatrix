Mumbai: BMC Speeds Up Sion Railway Overbridge, Western Side By May, Full Completion By July 2026

After criticism from the civic standing committee over delays, the BMC has accelerated the Sion Railway Overbridge project. A pedestrian underpass is now open, the western side is set to finish by May 31, and full completion is expected by July 15, 2026. Officials are coordinating with railways and traffic police to ensure timely execution.
