Sabarimala gold loss case: SIT to send samples to BARC, other premier labs for scientific analysis

In a latest development in the Sabarimala gold loss case, the SIT on Monday informed the Kerala High Court that samples of artefacts collected from the hill shrine would be sent to specialised agencies, including the BARC in Mumbai, for scientific analysis. The submission was made when a division bench comprising Justices Raja Vijayaraghavan and K V Jayakumar reviewed the progress of the investigation.
