HAL issues statement on Tejas fighter jet crash at Dubai Air Show

Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd (HAL) has expressed grief over the death of Wing Commander Namansh Syal, who lost his life when an LCA Tejas crashed during an aerial display at the Dubai Air Show. The incident marks the second Tejas crash in 20 months.
