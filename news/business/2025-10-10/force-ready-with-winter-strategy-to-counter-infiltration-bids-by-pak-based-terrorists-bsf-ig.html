Force ready with winter strategy to counter infiltration bids by Pak-based terrorists: BSF IG

The Border Security Force is prepared for winter infiltration attempts from Pakistan. Intelligence suggests terror groups are regrouping. The BSF's winter strategy is in place to thwart any attempts to cross the border, especially during foggy conditions. Forces are vigilant and ready to respond to any enemy action. The BSF is also enhancing its drone warfare capabilities.
