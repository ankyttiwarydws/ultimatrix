Amid age relaxation row, JKPSC chairperson says CCE to proceed as scheduled

JKPSC will hold the Combined Competitive Examination (CCE) as scheduled despite Chief Minister Abdullah's plea to postpone it due to travel disruptions and age relaxation uncertainty. The Lieutenant Governor returned the age relaxation file, citing no government response to his query about exam feasibility. Political parties and aspirants advocate for postponement.
