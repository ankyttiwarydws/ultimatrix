NCLT Declines Relief To Byjus, Refuses To Stay Aakash EGM On Rights Issue

The National Company Law Tribunal has declined to grant any interim relief to insolvency-bound edtech firm Byju's, which sought to restrain Aakash Educational Services Ltd (AESL) from convening its extraordinary general meeting (EGM) for a rights issue.
