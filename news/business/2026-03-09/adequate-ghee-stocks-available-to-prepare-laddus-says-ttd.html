Adequate ghee stocks available to prepare laddus, says TTD

Tirupati laddu ghee: TTD clarified that despite a tanker accident, ghee stocks from other suppliers are sufficient. Laddu prasadam preparation and distribution continue smoothly, with seven lakh laddus ready for devotees during the peak summer season.
