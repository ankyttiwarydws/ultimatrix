Air India, AI Express to operate 60 flights connecting Middle East cities on Tue

Airlines are flying to the Middle East despite conflict. Air India and Air India Express will run 60 flights, including 32 special services to the UAE. Akasa Air adjusts its schedule. IndiGo has flown many customers and will operate 38 flights. SpiceJet is running special flights from Dubai. Delays are possible for some international flights.
