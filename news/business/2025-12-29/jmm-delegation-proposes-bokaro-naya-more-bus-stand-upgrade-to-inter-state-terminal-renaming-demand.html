JMM Delegation Proposes Bokaro Naya More Bus Stand Upgrade to Inter-State Terminal, Renaming Demand

A Jharkhand Mukti Morcha delegation urged Chief Minister Hemant Soren to upgrade Bokaro's Naya More bus stand into an inter-state terminal. They also proposed renaming it after Shibu Soren, believing the project will enhance passenger facilities, regional connectivity, and economic growth. The Chief Minister visited Bokaro airport briefly.
