UP CM Yogi Adityanath inaugurates Shree Cement plant in Etah; highlights state's transformation & growth

Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath opened Shree Cement's factory in Etah. He spoke about Uttar Pradesh's progress under the BJP. Adityanath mentioned the state's strict approach to crime. He also criticised previous governments for encouraging criminal activity. The Chief Minister stated that Uttar Pradesh has significantly improved its ranking among Indian states.
