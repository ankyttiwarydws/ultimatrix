ISRO Completes Nearly Twice as Many Missions Since 2015: Chairman V Narayanan

ISRO Chairman V Narayanan announced a doubling of missions between 2015-2025 compared to the previous decade. He praised astronaut Shubhanshu Shukla's safe return from the ISS. Upcoming missions include launching a 6,500 kg US satellite and G20 satellites.
