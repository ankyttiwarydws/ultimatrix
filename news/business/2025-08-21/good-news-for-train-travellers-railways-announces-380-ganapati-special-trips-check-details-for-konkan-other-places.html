Good News for train travellers! Railways announces 380 Ganapati special trips — Check details for Konkan, other places

Good News for travellers! Ministry of Railways announced the operation of 380 Ganpati Special train trips for 2025, with destinations including Udupi, Kolad, and the Konkan region, among other places. The Ganpati Puja will be celebrated from 27 August 2025 to 6 September 2025.
