Additional levy over 40% on sin/de-merit goods proposed: West Bengal FM at GoM meet

As the Centre pushes for a simpler two-slab GST structure, West Bengal Finance Minister Chandrima Bhattacharya has suggested an extra levy above 40% on sin and de-merit goods. The revamped regime, expected by Diwali, would tax most items at either 5% or 18%.
