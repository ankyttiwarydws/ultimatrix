US exempts agri products from tariffs, India to gain small edge: GTRI

India may see a small trade advantage as the US exempts agricultural goods like spices, tea, and coffee from reciprocal tariffs, a GTRI report said. However, gains are limited as India's current exports in these areas are comparatively small.
