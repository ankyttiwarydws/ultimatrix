European Stocks Close On Strong Note Amid Hopes Of U.S.-Iran Peace Deal

European stocks closed on a strong note on Wednesday, as the mood turned a bit bullish amid some hopes of a de-escalation in tensions in the Middle East. Despite rejecting the US peace plan as excessive and issued five conditions to end the war, Iran said non-hostile vessels may transit the Strait of Hormuz with Tehran's consent.
