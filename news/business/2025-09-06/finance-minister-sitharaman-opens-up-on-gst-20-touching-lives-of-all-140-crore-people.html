Finance Minister Sitharaman opens up on GST 2.0, touching ‘lives of all 140 crore people’

Nearly 400 products - from soaps to cars, shampoos to tractors and air conditioners - will cost less when the rejig of the GST is effective from the first day of Navaratri on September 22. Premium paid on individual health and life insurance will be tax-free.
