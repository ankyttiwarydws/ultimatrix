Rice exporters call GST reform economic catalyst; agri sector gains from quicker refunds, consumers to benefit

Next-generation GST reforms are poised to revolutionize India's economy, particularly benefiting agriculture and rice exports. Reduced GST registration times, quicker refunds, and rate cuts are expected to stimulate demand and unlock working capital for the rice industry. With a record rice harvest anticipated, these reforms will empower consumers and ensure fair prices for farmers.
