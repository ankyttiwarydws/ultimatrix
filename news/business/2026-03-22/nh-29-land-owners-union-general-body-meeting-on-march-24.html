NH-29 land owners union general body meeting on March 24

Chümoukedima, March 22 (MExN): The Land Owners Union, NH-29 (Purana Bazaar to Chümoukedima), will hold its general body meeting on March 24 at the residence of the Legal Advisor, Zhimo Vila, C/33, Block-3, 5th Mile, Unity Village, Chümoukedima, at 10 am.According to a notice, the meeting will deliberate on crucial agendas, and all concerned members have been requested to attend without fail.
