PNG supply to restaurants, eateries increased by 20% in Maharashtra

Maharashtra Food and Civil Supplies Minister Chhagan Bhujbal on Sunday said the supply of piped natural gas to commercial establishments has been increased by 20%, offering relief to restaurants and eateries.
