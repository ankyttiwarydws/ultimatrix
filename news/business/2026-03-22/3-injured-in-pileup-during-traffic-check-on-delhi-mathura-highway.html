3 injured in pileup during traffic check on Delhi-Mathura highway

A chain collision involving a Swift, WagonR, and a truck injured three people on the Delhi-Mathura Highway near Bata Metro Station. The pile-up occurred during a traffic police lane violation challaning drive when a Swift braked suddenly, leading to subsequent impacts. A pillion rider on a motorcycle also sustained injuries.
