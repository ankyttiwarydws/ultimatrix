HM Amit Shah to inaugurate Startup Conclave 2025 in Gandhinagar on Sep 23

Gandhinagar, Sep 21, 2025 Union Home Minister Amit Shah will inaugurate the Startup Conclave 2025 at Mahatma Mandir in Gandhinagar on Tuesday. The two-day event, organised by the Gujarat government’s Education department, will see the participation of Chief Minister Bhupendra Patel and the State Higher and Technical Education Minister Rushikesh Patel at the opening ceremony.
