Jharkhand government allocates Rs 48.7 crore for modernization of key bus terminals in Ranchi

Ranchi's three key bus terminals are set for a major upgrade. The state government has sanctioned Rs 48.7 crore for modernization. This will bring state-of-the-art amenities for thousands of daily passengers. Plans include spacious waiting areas, dormitories, and improved traffic flow. The redevelopment aims to provide much-needed relief and dignity to commuters.
