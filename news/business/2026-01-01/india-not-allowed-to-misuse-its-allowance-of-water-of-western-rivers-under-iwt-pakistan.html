India not allowed to misuse its allowance of water of western rivers under IWT: Pakistan

Pakistan has expressed serious concerns over India's reported plans for a new hydropower project on the Chenab River. Islamabad stated that India cannot misuse its water allowance under the Indus Waters Treaty. Pakistan has sought clarification from India regarding the project's details. The treaty governs water distribution between the two nations.
