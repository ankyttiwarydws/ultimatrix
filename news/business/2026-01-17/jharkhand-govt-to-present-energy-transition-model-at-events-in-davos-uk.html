Jharkhand govt to present energy transition model at events in Davos, UK

During his upcoming engagements at the World Economic Forum in Davos and in the United Kingdom, Jharkhand Chief Minister Hemant Soren will present an innovative model for energy transition. This strategy hinges on responsibly diversifying the state's energy portfolio by enhancing existing infrastructure and investing in cutting-edge solar and clean technologies.
