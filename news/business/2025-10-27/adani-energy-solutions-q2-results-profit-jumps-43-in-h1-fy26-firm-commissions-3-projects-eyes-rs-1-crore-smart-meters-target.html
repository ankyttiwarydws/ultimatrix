Adani Energy Solutions Q2 results: Profit jumps 43% in H1 FY26; firm commissions 3 projects, eyes Rs 1 crore smart meters target

Adani Energy Solutions reported robust H1 FY26 results, with total income up 16.4% to Rs 13,793 crore and EBITDA rising 13.4%. The company commissioned three new transmission lines and achieved a record 74 lakh smart meter installations, underscoring strong operational execution and a significant growth pipeline.
