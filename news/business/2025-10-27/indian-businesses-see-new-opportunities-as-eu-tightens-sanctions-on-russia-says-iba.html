Indian businesses see new opportunities as EU tightens sanctions on Russia, says IBA

New European Union sanctions on Russia are opening doors for Indian businesses. Local production and innovation in Russia are increasing. India's trade with Russia has reached a record USD 68.7 billion. Indian companies are supplying essential goods and machinery. This cooperation benefits both nations with stable product access and growing markets.
