For $10 trillion GDP by 2035, India needs to draw on patient capital: Spark Capital CEO Rama Rao at ET Now GBS

India needs patient capital to achieve its $10 trillion GDP target by 2035, according to Spark Capital's Y Rama Rao. He highlighted the success of South Korea and Singapore in their decades of disruption through strategic investments in education and infrastructure, urging India to focus on asset creation and long-term growth over short-term returns.
