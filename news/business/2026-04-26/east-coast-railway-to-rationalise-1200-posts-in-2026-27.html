East Coast Railway to rationalise 1,200+ posts in 2026-27

East Coast Railway will rationalize over 1,200 posts by 2026-27 as part of a nationwide manpower optimization drive. This initiative, targeting about 2% of its sanctioned strength, involves redistributing surplus staff and surrendering posts. The exercise aims to enhance efficiency while redeploying personnel to critical operational areas like safety and maintenance.
