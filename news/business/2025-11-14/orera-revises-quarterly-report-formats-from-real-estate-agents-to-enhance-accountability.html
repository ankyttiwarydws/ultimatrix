ORERA revises quarterly report formats from real estate agents to enhance accountability

Odisha Real Estate Regulatory Authority (ORERA) has mandated a revised format for quarterly progress reports from registered agents to enhance accountability and streamline disclosures. This move follows an audit observation highlighting widespread non-compliance and insufficient data in agent filings. The updated proforma requires detailed transaction-level information, aiming to plug data gaps and ensure transparency in the property market.
