Govt To Propose Bill For Hiking Insurance Sector FDI To 100% In Parliament's Winter Session

In the upcoming winter session of the Parliament, the Government of India plans to introduce a bill to raise foreign direct investment (FDI) in the insurance sector to 100%, according to reports. Parliament's winter session will begin from Dec. 1, 2025 and will end on Dec. 19. It will have 15 working days.
