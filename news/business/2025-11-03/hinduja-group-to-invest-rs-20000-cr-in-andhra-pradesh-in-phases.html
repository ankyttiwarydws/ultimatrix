Hinduja group to invest Rs 20,000 cr in Andhra Pradesh in phases

Amaravati: Diversified conglomerate Hinduja Group agreed to invest Rs 20,000 crore in Andhra Pradesh in phases, following a meeting with Chief Minister N Chandrababu Naidu in London, the state government said on Monday.
