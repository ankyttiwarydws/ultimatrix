Grievance cell in Gumla for LPG supply plaints

The Gumla administration has taken a significant step forward by establishing a grievance redressal cell focused on resolving issues related to LPG supply. This initiative allows citizens to report any complaints daily, from 10 am to 5 pm, with the exception of Sundays and holidays.
