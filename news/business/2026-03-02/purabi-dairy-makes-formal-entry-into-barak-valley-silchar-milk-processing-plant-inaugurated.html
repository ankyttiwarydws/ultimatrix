Purabi Dairy makes formal entry into Barak Valley, Silchar milk processing plant inaugurated

A renovated dairy processing plant in Silchar has been inaugurated, bringing organized cooperative dairying to Barak Valley. The plant, upgraded with new machinery, will process 20,000 litres of milk daily under the Purabi brand. This development aims to provide quality milk to consumers and create stable income for local farmers.
