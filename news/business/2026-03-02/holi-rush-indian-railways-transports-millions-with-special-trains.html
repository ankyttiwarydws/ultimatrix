Holi Rush: Indian Railways Transports Millions with Special Trains

Indian Railways successfully managed the Holi festive rush, transporting millions of people home. Northern Railways handled 1.4 million passengers in five days, and Western Railway catered to 2.3 million, using special trains and on-ground support.
