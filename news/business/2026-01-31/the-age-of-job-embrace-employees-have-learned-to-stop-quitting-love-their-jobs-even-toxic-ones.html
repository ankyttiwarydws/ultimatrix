The age of job embrace: Employees have learned to stop quitting & love their jobs, even toxic ones

Workers are now holding onto their jobs tightly. This is a change from the past when many people switched jobs frequently. Economic worries and fewer job opportunities are making employees feel insecure. They prefer the known challenges of their current roles over the risks of finding new employment. This trend is impacting the job market globally, including in India.
