Reliance stops importing Russian crude for refinery operations

Reliance Industries said it has stopped processing Russian crude at its Jamnagar SEZ refinery from 20 November, moving fully to non-Russian crude for all product exports starting 1 December. The company said the shift was completed ahead of schedule to meet product-import restrictions that take effect on 21 January 2026.
