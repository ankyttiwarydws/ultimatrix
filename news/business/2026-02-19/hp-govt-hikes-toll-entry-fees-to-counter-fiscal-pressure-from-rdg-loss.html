HP govt hikes toll, entry fees to counter fiscal pressure from RDG loss

To counter fiscal pressure from the discontinuation of the Revenue Deficit Grant, Himachal Pradesh has introduced reforms. This includes a new e-auction-based toll policy, revised vehicle entry fees, and changes to its excise framework.
