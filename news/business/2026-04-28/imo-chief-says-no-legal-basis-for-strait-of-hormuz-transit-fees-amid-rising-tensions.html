IMO Chief Says No Legal Basis for Strait of Hormuz Transit Fees Amid Rising Tensions

The head of the International Maritime Organization (IMO) has firmly stated that there is “no legal basis” for imposing any fees on ships transiting the strategically vital Strait of Hormuz, even as tensions continue to disrupt global shipping routes.
