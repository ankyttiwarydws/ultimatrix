India, UAE unveil roadmap to double trade, expand nuclear cooperation

Following UAE President Al Nahyan's visit, India and UAE set a target to double bilateral trade to over USD 200 billion by 2032, expand cooperation in advanced nuclear technologies, and signed multiple agreements on investment, energy, and tech.
