Jammu Rail Division Fines Ticketless Passengers ₹32 Lakh in 10 Days

The Jammu Railway Division has fined at least 2500 passengers for travelling without tickets in the past 10 days during the festive season, collecting Rs 32 lakh, a senior official said on Saturday.
