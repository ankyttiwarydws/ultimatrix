Delhi govt assures normal fuel supply, urges residents not to panic

The Delhi government has refuted rumours of a fuel shortage, confirming that the supply of LPG, petrol, diesel, and PNG remains normal. Officials urge residents not to panic-buy or hoard, assuring that the supply chain is stable and secure.
