Govt holds high-level meet to tackle India's shipping challenges

The government held a high-level meeting with the National Shipping Board, chaired by Sarbananda Sonowal, to tackle challenges in India's shipping sector due to global issues and to review measures for strengthening maritime capacity and trade.
