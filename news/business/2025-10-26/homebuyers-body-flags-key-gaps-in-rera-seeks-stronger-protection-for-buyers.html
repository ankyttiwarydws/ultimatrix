Homebuyers’ body flags key gaps in RERA, seeks stronger protection for buyers

A homebuyers' association has urged the Union housing ministry to address critical RERA Act gaps, citing ongoing buyer exploitation. They seek Standard Operating Procedures to ensure developers complete promised amenities, verify promoter track records, and provide a clear exit policy for buyers, restoring trust in the sector.
