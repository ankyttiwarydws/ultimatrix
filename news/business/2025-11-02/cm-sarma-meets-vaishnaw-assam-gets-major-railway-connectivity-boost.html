CM Sarma meets Vaishnaw; Assam gets major railway connectivity boost

Assam CM Himanta Biswa Sarma met Railway Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw, securing approval for 3 new Amrit Bharat trains, a new Umrangso-Lanka rail link, and faster work on the Kokrajhar-Gelephu line to Bhutan to boost connectivity in the state.
