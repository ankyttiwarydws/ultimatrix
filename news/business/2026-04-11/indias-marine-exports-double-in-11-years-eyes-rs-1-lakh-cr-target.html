India's marine exports double in 11 years, eyes Rs 1 lakh Cr target

India's marine product exports have doubled in the last 11 years to Rs 62,408 crore in 2024-25. At the Seafood Exporters Meet 2026, the government urged exporters to aim for a Rs 1 lakh crore target and focus on market diversification.
