Hexagon Nutrition Files Draft Papers With SEBI For IPO

Hexagon Nutrition Ltd. filed its preliminary papers with the capital markets regulator SEBI on Monday to raise funds via an initial public offering. The public offer will include only an offer for sale segment of 3.08 crore shares. The equity shares are proposed to be listed on the National Stock Exchange of India Ltd and BSE Ltd.
