Number Guessing
About Number Guessing

Play the Number Guessing game online! Try to guess the secret number between 1 and 100. This simple and fun game is perfect for practicing logic, deduction, and number sense. Great for kids and adults alike!

How to Play:
  • Enter your guess in the input box and click Submit.
  • The game will tell you if your guess is too high, too low, or correct.
  • Keep guessing until you find the secret number!
