About Typing Speed Test

Take the Typing Speed Test online! Type the words as fast and accurately as you can. This game helps improve your typing speed, accuracy, and focus. Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone looking to boost their keyboard skills.

How to Play:
  • Type the word shown in the box as quickly as possible.
  • When you type the word correctly, a new word appears and your time is recorded.
  • Try to improve your speed and accuracy with each round!
