About Whack-a-Mole

Play Whack-a-Mole online for free! Test your reflexes and reaction speed by hitting the moles as they pop up. This classic arcade game is fun, fast-paced, and perfect for a quick break.

How to Play:
  • Click on the moles as soon as they appear in the holes.
  • Each successful hit increases your score.
  • Try to get the highest score before time runs out!
