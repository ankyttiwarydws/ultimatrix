Marari Beach: The Essence of Coastal Simplicity

For those craving peace, Marari Beach near Alleppey is a tranquil haven. Fringed by swaying palms and sleepy fishing villages, this stretch of sand is the opposite of crowded and commercialized. Here, time seems to slow down. Watch fishermen haul in their catch, cycle through coconut groves, or simply relax in a hammock as gentle waves lap the shore.
Boutique resorts and eco-friendly homestays offer authentic Kerala hospitality, with plenty of opportunities for yoga, Ayurvedic treatments, and delicious seafood.
Why go: Secluded sands, village life experiences, and restful luxury with a low-key vibe.
