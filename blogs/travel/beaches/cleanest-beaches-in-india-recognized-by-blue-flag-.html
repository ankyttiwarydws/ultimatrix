How Does a Beach Qualify for Blue Flag?

To become Blue Flag certified, a beach must comply with 33 strict criteria which cover:

  • Water quality (regular laboratory testing)
  • Environmental management (waste, recycling, natural habitat protection)
  • Safety and services (lifeguards, first aid, accessibility for all)
  • Environmental education (awareness activities, informative signage)
