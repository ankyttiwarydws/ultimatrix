1. Beas Kund, Himachal Pradesh

Beas Kund is a breathtaking alpine lake nestled among the snowy peaks near Manali. The trek to Beas Kund is renowned for its lush meadows, wildflowers in bloom, and dramatic views of the surrounding Himalayan giants. This spot is ideal for those who enjoy moderate trekking and wish to experience pristine scenery away from crowds. The route is accessible to beginners, with options to trek solo or join a group. The best time to visit is from May to June, when the valley bursts with color and life.
Highlights: Trekking, camping, panoramic mountain views, wildflowers.

2. Parvati Valley & Kheerganga, Himachal Pradesh

Parvati Valley is famed for its mystical landscapes, quaint villages, and the legendary Kheerganga trek. The valley boasts lush green hills, gushing rivers, and natural hot springs, making it a sanctuary for both adventure seekers and those longing for peaceful immersion in nature. The trek to Kheerganga rewards travelers with a soak in thermal springs set against the backdrop of rugged mountains. Visit between April-June or September-November for the best experience.
Highlights: Trekking, hot springs, village life, breathtaking views.

3. Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

Ziro Valley is a UNESCO World Heritage candidate, known for its rolling hills, verdant rice fields, and unique Apatani tribal culture. The valley is especially stunning in October when the landscape transforms post-harvest, offering a patchwork of vibrant greenery and clear blue skies. Ziro is perfect for those seeking solitude, cultural immersion, and scenic beauty.
Highlights: Rice fields, tribal culture, music festivals, peaceful environment.

4. Pelling, Sikkim

Pelling is a tranquil mountain town with panoramic vistas of the Kanchenjunga range. Less crowded than Gangtok, Pelling invites visitors to explore ancient monasteries, cascading waterfalls, and the historic Rabdentse Ruins. The area is rich in biodiversity and offers a blend of nature, spirituality, and history.
Highlights: Kanchenjunga views, monasteries, waterfalls, heritage sites.
