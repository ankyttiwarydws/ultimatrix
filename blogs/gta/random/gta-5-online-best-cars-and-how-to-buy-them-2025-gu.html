How to Buy the Best Cars in GTA Online

Purchasing cars in GTA Online is straightforward, but knowing where to buy and how to unlock upgrades is key:

  • Legendary Motorsport: For supercars and sports cars, visit this in-game website on your phone.
  • Southern San Andreas Super Autos: Hosts muscle cars, off-roaders, and some sports cars.
  • Warstock Cache & Carry: Buy weaponized vehicles and special rides like the Vigilante.
  • Upgrading: Visit Los Santos Customs or the LS Car Meet for performance and visual upgrades, including HSW and Imani Tech.
  • Special Unlocks: Some cars and upgrades are exclusive to next-gen consoles or require returning player status. Always check requirements before purchasing.

Tips for Maximizing Your Investment

Choose vehicles that fit your playstyle: Speed for racing, Imani Tech for protection, weaponized for missions.

Save for HSW upgrades: These can dramatically improve speed and handling but require extra cash and access to the LS Car Meet.

Customize for fun and utility: Visual upgrades let you stand out, while performance mods help you win races and survive in free roam.

Watch for discounts and events: Rockstar frequently discounts vehicles during weekly updates, so you can save millions if you time your purchases right.
