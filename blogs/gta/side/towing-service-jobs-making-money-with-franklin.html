What Does a Tow Truck Driver Do?

Tow truck drivers are responsible for safely transporting vehicles that are damaged, disabled, illegally parked, or require roadside assistance. The job involves operating specialized trucks, ensuring safety on the road, and often dealing with stressful situations. A typical day might include responding to emergency calls, navigating city streets, and interacting with customers who need help.
Those who excel in this role tend to be calm under pressure, skilled at driving large vehicles, and able to manage paperwork and customer service.

How Much Money Can You Make in Towing Services?
