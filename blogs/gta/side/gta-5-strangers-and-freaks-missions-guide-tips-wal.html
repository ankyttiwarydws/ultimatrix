GTA 5 Strangers and Freaks Missions Guide

Introduction to Strangers and Freaks Missions

The Strangers and Freaks missions in Grand Theft Auto V are a collection of quirky, memorable side quests scattered throughout San Andreas. Marked by a "?" icon on your map, these encounters introduce you to some of the most eccentric characters in the game. While these missions are technically optional, completing select ones is crucial for achieving 100% game completion[1][2][3].

Why Complete Strangers and Freaks Missions?

Completing these missions not only adds depth to the game's narrative but is also essential for unlocking achievements and reaching 100% completion. Gold Medal Objectives and Replayability

Like main story missions, Strangers and Freaks quests award a bronze, silver, or gold medal based on your performance. Gold medals require meeting specific criteria, such as completing a mission within a time limit or accomplishing bonus objectives. You can replay most missions to improve your medal rating, which is important for trophy hunters and completionists[3][5].

Tips for Completing Strangers and Freaks Missions Efficiently

  • Use the Right Character: Many missions are exclusive to a specific protagonist. Switch between Michael, Franklin, and Trevor to see all available opportunities on your map[1].

Sample Mission Walkthrough: Pulling Favors (Tonya)

One of the earliest Strangers and Freaks missions is Pulling Favors for Tonya. Franklin meets Tonya in Strawberry, drives to a tow truck, recovers an abandoned car, and delivers it to the impound lot. To earn a gold medal, complete the mission in under five minutes and keep the vehicle hooked until delivery. This mission is required for 100% completion[5].
