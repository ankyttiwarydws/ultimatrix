High Risk: The Dangers for Robbers

While the potential reward is high, so are the risks. Armored trucks are fortified vehicles, and their guards are usually armed and trained to respond to threats. The risk of violent confrontation is significant, with law enforcement agencies prioritizing these crimes due to the large sums involved and the danger to public safety. Why the Surge? Economic and Social Factors

Experts point to several possible reasons for the recent increase. Economic uncertainty, rising inflation, and the ongoing digitalization of financial transactions have put more cash in transit, creating more opportunities for criminals. Additionally, organized crime groups have become more sophisticated, studying security routines and exploiting vulnerabilities. The surge in robberies is not just a reflection of criminal daring, but of the evolving tactics and desperation seen in parts of the criminal underworld[1][3].
