The Most Popular Motorcycle Stunts and Tricks

Modern stunt riding is a showcase of creativity and skill. Here are some of the most famous and visually stunning tricks performed by today‚Äôs top riders:

  • Wheelie: Balancing on the rear wheel while moving forward. It‚Äôs often the first trick aspiring stunters learn, but mastering it takes hours of practice and precision[3][1].

  • Burnout: Spinning the rear tire while keeping the bike stationary, creating clouds of smoke and a roar of spinning rubber[3].
