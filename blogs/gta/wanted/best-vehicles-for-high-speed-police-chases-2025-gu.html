What Makes a Great Police Pursuit Vehicle?

Pursuit-rated police vehicles are engineered for more than just speed. They must offer excellent handling, quick acceleration, robust braking systems, and safety features tailored to the unpredictable nature of chases. Reliability, comfort, and up-to-date technology also play crucial roles in keeping officers and the public safe during high-stress situations.
