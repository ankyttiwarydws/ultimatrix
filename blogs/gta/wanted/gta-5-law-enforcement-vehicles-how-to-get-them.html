GTA 5 Law Enforcement Vehicles: How to Get Them

Law enforcement vehicles are among the most iconic rides in Grand Theft Auto V, from classic police cruisers to armored patrol SUVs. Whether you want to roleplay as a cop, complete missions, or simply enjoy driving these robust cars around Los Santos, knowing how to obtain them is essential. This guide covers everything you need to know about getting police vehicles in GTA 5, both in Story Mode and GTA Online.

Types of Law Enforcement Vehicles in GTA 5

GTA 5 features a variety of law enforcement vehicles, each with unique designs, performance stats, and uses. Some of the most popular and recognizable police vehicles include:

  • Police Cruiser: The standard cop car, often seen patrolling city streets. There are several versions based on real-life sedans and muscle cars.
  • Police Roadcruiser: A newer addition with a tested top speed of 101.50 mph, available in GTA Online.
  • Canis Terminus Patrol: An armored SUV inspired by the Jeep Wrangler, with a top speed of 119.50 mph. Highly durable and suitable for tough missions.
  • Bravado Police Gauntlet Interceptor: Based on the Dodge Challenger, this police car boasts a top speed of 122.00 mph and excellent handling.
  • Sheriff Cruiser & SUV: Used by county sheriffs, these vehicles are inspired by the Ford Crown Victoria and Chevy Suburban.
  • FIB Buffalo & Granger: Special law enforcement variants used in federal missions and high-speed chases.
