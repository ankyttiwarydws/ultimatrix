GTA 5 Cheat Codes List for PC, Xbox, and PlayStation

If you’ve ever played Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5), you know the thrill of unleashing chaos and creativity in Los Santos. But did you know you can take it to the next level using cheat codes? Whether you’re on PC, Xbox, or PlayStation, cheat codes let you access special powers, weapons, vehicles, and more. This guide brings you the complete list of GTA 5 cheat codes and shows you how to activate them on your platform.

How to Enter Cheat Codes in GTA 5

GTA 5 makes it easy to use cheats, but the method varies by platform:

  • PC: Open the console by pressing the tilde (~) key, then type the cheat code and hit Enter. You can also use your in-game phone for certain cheats.
  • Xbox & PlayStation: Enter the specific button combinations on your controller during gameplay (don’t pause the game). Alternatively, use your character’s cell phone to dial cheat numbers.

Note: Cheats only work in single-player mode, not in GTA Online. Activating cheats will disable achievements/trophies temporarily for that session.
