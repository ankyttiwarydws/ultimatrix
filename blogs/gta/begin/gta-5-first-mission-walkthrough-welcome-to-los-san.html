Prologue Recap: Setting the Stage

Before you hit the streets of Los Santos, GTA 5 opens with a flashback heist in North Yankton. You'll control Michael Townley and Trevor Philips as they rob a bank. This action-packed sequence introduces you to the game's cover system, basic gunplay, and character-switching mechanic. Once the prologue concludes, the story fast-forwards to the sun-soaked city of Los Santos, where the main game begins.

Welcome to Los Santos: Mission Objectives

As the title card fades in, you meet Franklin Clinton and his friend Lamar Davis, working as repo men for a luxury car dealership. Their boss, Simeon Yetarian, sends them out to repossess two high-end vehicles. Your objective is to help Franklin steal a white sports car, teaching you the fundamentals of driving and pursuit.

  • Drive to the House: Follow Lamar to the marked location on the map. This is your first chance to get a feel for Los Santos' sprawling neighborhoods and the game's responsive driving controls.
  • Steal the Car: Once you arrive, sneak onto the property, find the car, and drive it away. The game introduces stealth mechanics and basic climbing here.
  • Race Back to the Dealership: A friendly (but intense) race with Lamar back to Simeon's dealership gives you a sense of the game's speed, handling, and the importance of avoiding police attention.
  • Return the Car: After the race, park the car at the dealership. This completes your first mission and unlocks new storylines and activities.
