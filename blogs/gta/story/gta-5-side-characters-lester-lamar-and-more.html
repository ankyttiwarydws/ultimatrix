GTA 5 Side Characters: Lester, Lamar, and More

Grand Theft Auto V is a masterpiece not just for its sprawling open world and explosive action, but also for its unforgettable cast of characters. While Michael, Franklin, and Trevor steal the spotlight, it’s the supporting players—like Lester Crest, Lamar Davis, and others—who add humor, depth, and crucial plot twists to the story. These side characters bring Los Santos to life, making every mission and cutscene memorable. Let’s shine a light on the personalities who make GTA 5’s world so rich and dynamic.

Lester Crest: The Mastermind in the Shadows

Lester Crest is the brains behind the game’s most audacious heists. Frail in body but unmatched in intellect, Lester is a hacker, planner, and trusted confidant to Michael, Trevor, and Franklin. His paranoia and dry wit make him a fan favorite, but it’s his strategic mind that truly sets him apart.

Lester’s backstory is deeply tied to the main trio. He was Michael’s remote contact during their early criminal days, orchestrating heists from afar. After Michael’s supposed death, Lester remained in the shadows until the crew reunited in Los Santos. He never betrayed Michael, keeping his secrets even at personal risk, and was quick to resume his role as their invaluable advisor when called upon again in 2013. Lester’s involvement doesn’t stop with the main story—he’s also a linchpin in GTA Online, helping players set up criminal empires and elaborate heists, sometimes even using a voice changer as “Long John Teabag” to protect his identity[2][3].

Lamar Davis: The Comic Relief with Heart

If Franklin is the grounded protagonist, Lamar Davis is the wild card. As Franklin’s best friend, Lamar is often the source of comic relief, but he’s also fiercely loyal and ambitious in his own right. He introduces Franklin to new opportunities and, of course, to Chop, the lovable rottweiler who quickly became a GTA icon.

Lamar’s tall stature (he’s a towering 6’7”) and his tendency to flip off cops rather than shoot them set him apart from the game’s more ruthless criminals. While he may not always make the smartest choices, Lamar’s energy and persistence are infectious. He provides a street-level view of Los Santos’ criminal underworld and is a constant presence throughout Franklin’s rise[4].

Other Memorable Side Characters

GTA 5’s world is populated by a colorful supporting cast that keeps players engaged at every turn:

  • Devin Weston: An arrogant business tycoon and the game’s primary antagonist, Devin uses his wealth and power to manipulate the protagonists, sending waves of trouble their way. His ruthless ambition makes him one of the most despised villains in the series[4].
  • Simeon Yetarian: Franklin’s shady car dealership boss, Simeon is as slippery as they come. His missions are often the first taste of Los Santos’ criminal life for new players, and his over-the-top personality is unforgettable.
  • Gerald: A street-level drug dealer who hands out jobs to up-and-coming criminals, Gerald provides players with early missions and a glimpse into the city’s gang rivalries.
  • Chop: More than just a pet, Chop helps Franklin track down targets and adds a unique, lighthearted touch to otherwise dangerous situations.

How Side Characters Shape the GTA 5 Experience

These side characters aren’t just window dressing—they actively shape the world of Los Santos. Their missions, personalities, and backstories add layers to the main narrative, offering players new perspectives and challenges. Whether you’re orchestrating a heist with Lester, chasing a score with Lamar, or navigating the treacherous waters of Los Santos’ elite with Devin Weston, these characters make every moment in GTA 5 unpredictable and entertaining.

Even in GTA Online, characters like Lester and Lamar continue to play pivotal roles. They offer new jobs, share hidden lore, and help players build their own criminal empires. This interconnectedness between story and online play is part of what makes GTA 5 such an enduring success[1][2].
