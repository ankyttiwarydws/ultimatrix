Choosing an Approach: Offshore vs. Freighter

Early in the mission, Lester offers two plans for stealing the device:

  • Offshore Approach: Michael and Trevor use a stolen helicopter to airlift a submarine into the ocean, with Trevor piloting the sub to retrieve the device. This is a high-adrenaline, action-packed route that involves aerial maneuvers and underwater navigation.
  • Freighter Approach: The team infiltrates a docked cargo ship. Franklin provides sniper support while Michael stealthily plants explosives and retrieves the device. This method focuses more on stealth and precision.

While both approaches deliver the same story outcome and no monetary reward, they offer different gameplay experiences and Gold Medal objectives. The choice is up to you‚Äîselect the one that sounds most fun or challenging.

Walkthrough: Freighter Approach

The Freighter plan is more tactical. Franklin takes out guards from a distance with a sniper rifle, while Michael sneaks through the ship, planting bombs and taking enemies by surprise. Once the charges are set, detonate them to sink the freighter and retrieve the device underwater. This approach rewards players who excel at stealth, accuracy, and fast takedowns.
