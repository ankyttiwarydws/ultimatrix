What Are Hidden Packages in GTA 5?

Hidden packages are collectible briefcases filled with cash, usually located underwater near wrecked planes, ships, or vehicles. They're designed as a little bonus for players who love exploration and want to boost their in-game wealth. Most of the packages contain between $7,000 and $25,000 each, making the total haul significant if you track them all down.
Keep in mind: 11 out of 12 hidden packages are underwater, so make sure you have access to scuba gear, a Dinghy, or a submersible like the Minisub for the deepest dives.[1]

Essential Gear for Your Underwater Search

To safely and efficiently collect every hidden package, make sure you have:

  • Scuba suit or diving gear: Available after completing certain missions or by using a submersible.
  • Dinghy or Minisub: Both can transport you directly to deep-water locations and come equipped with diving equipment.[2]
  • Sniper rifle or binoculars (optional): Helps you spot wrecks from the surface before you dive.
