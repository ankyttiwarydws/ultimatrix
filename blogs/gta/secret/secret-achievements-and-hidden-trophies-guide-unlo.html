What Are Secret Achievements and Hidden Trophies?

Secret achievements and hidden trophies are special objectives in games that are concealed from the standard achievement list. Their descriptions are often hidden to prevent spoilers or to encourage players to explore and experiment. Unlocking them can involve anything from defeating an unknown boss, performing quirky actions, or finding obscure collectibles.

Why Do Developers Hide Achievements?

Game developers use hidden achievements to:

  • Prevent spoilers for major story events or twists.
  • Encourage exploration and replayability by rewarding curiosity.
  • Add humor or Easter eggs for those willing to go off the beaten path.
  • Challenge veteran players with tasks requiring skill, creativity, or persistence.

How to Find Hidden Trophies on PlayStation and Xbox

Modern consoles make it easier to hunt down hidden trophies. For example, on PlayStation 5, you can reveal hidden trophies in your trophy list by simply pressing the square button when viewing them, or by using the options menu to show all hidden details for a game. This allows you to see the requirements before you attempt to unlock them, making the process less mysterious but much more manageable for trophy hunters.
