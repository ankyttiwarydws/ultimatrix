GTA V: All Hidden Packages Locations

In Grand Theft Auto V, hidden packages take the form of briefcases filled with cash, usually found underwater. To collect all twelve, you'll need scuba gear or a submarine. Here are some highlights:

  • Paleto Cove Briefcase ($12,000): Found on the ocean floor near a detached airplane tail. Watch for wreckage as a landmark.
  • North Chumash Briefcase ($12,500): Inside the belly of a wrecked plane. Look for submarine parts nearby.
  • Zancudo Briefcase ($9,000): On top of a sunken ship, also containing weapon pickups and collectibles.
  • Pacific Bluffs Briefcase ($25,000): The most lucrative package, sitting atop a wrecked submarine near its hatch.
  • Los Santos Dock Briefcase ($11,000): Hidden inside a wrecked tanker ship, south of the LS Docks.

Liberty City Stories: All 100 Hidden Packages

GTA: Liberty City Stories challenges players to find 100 hidden packages scattered across the city. Many are cleverly concealed in construction sites, junkyards, behind dumpsters, or inside vehicles at garages.

  • Junkyard: Look behind tanks and near the crusher for well-hidden packages.
  • Construction sites: Check behind blue walls and inside unfinished buildings.
  • Vehicle shops: Packages can be found near or inside garages and offices.
  • Near ramps and train tracks: These are common hiding spots, sometimes requiring a keen eye to spot.

Pro tip: You can collect most packages early in the game, but some require story progress to unlock certain areas.
