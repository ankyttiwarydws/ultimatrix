Hidden Money Briefcases: Locations and Values

If you've spent any time exploring the vast world of Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5), you might have heard whispers of secret money briefcases hidden throughout Los Santos and Blaine County. These briefcases, also known as hidden packages, contain large sums of cash and can be found in some of the most secluded and challenging spots in the game. In this guide, we'll uncover where to find them, what they're worth, and how you can maximize your haul.

What Are Hidden Money Briefcases?

Hidden money briefcases in GTA 5 are collectible items that look like glowing silver or gray cases. When found, they reward the player with a specific amount of cash, ranging from $7,000 to as much as $25,000 per briefcase. Most of these are located underwater, and some require diving equipment like a scuba suit or minisub to access. There are also a few found on land, as well as others that appear during random in-game events.

How Many Hidden Briefcases Are There?

The majority of sources agree there are 12 main hidden money briefcases scattered throughout the map, with 11 located underwater and several more that may spawn during specific events or missions. To collect them efficiently, it's best to come prepared with the right gear and a solid plan for your treasure hunt.
Some reports suggest there are actually 16 total when including land-based briefcases and those tied to special scenarios[5].

Tips for Finding Hidden Briefcases

  • Bring Scuba Gear: Eleven out of the twelve main briefcases are underwater, and some are at considerable depth. Equip a scuba suit or use a minisub for easier and safer retrieval.
  • Use the Submarine: The minisub is perfect for deep dives and can help avoid dangerous sea creatures such as sharks.
  • Watch for Respawns: Briefcases may respawn after one in-game day, allowing you to collect them multiple times if youâ€™re patient[5].
  • Be Aware of Glitches: Sometimes, briefcases may not appear due to in-game bugs. If this happens, try saving and reloading your game, or use a different character.

How Much Can You Make?

Each hidden briefcase contains a different sum, but the total from collecting all standard hidden packages is at least $100,000. If you factor in respawns, random events, and repeatable locations, the potential is even higher. The Pacific Bluffs and Altruist Cult Shootout briefcases offer the highest single-case payouts, at $25,000 each.
