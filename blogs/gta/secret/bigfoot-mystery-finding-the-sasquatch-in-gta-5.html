The Peyote Plant Transformation: Becoming Bigfoot

The search for Sasquatch took a dramatic turn with the addition of the Peyote plant Easter egg. After a game update, players discovered a unique golden Peyote plant that, under very specific conditions, lets you transform into Bigfoot:

  • Weather must be foggy.
  • Time must be between 3:30 and 8:30 AM in-game.
  • The location is near Grapeseed, on the side of Mount Chiliad.

If you manage to find the plant under these conditions, you can experience the game as Sasquatchâ€”running, roaring, and causing mayhem across the map[3].

Unlocking Bigfoot in Director Mode

For those who want to play as Bigfoot outside the single transformation, eating the Peyote plant and experiencing the hallucination unlocks the Wild Animal (Bigfoot) in Director Mode. This allows you to select Sasquatch as a playable character and swap to "The Beast" using special controls[4].

The process involves seeking out the golden Peyote plants scattered around the map. Each plant offers a chance to transform into different animals, but only the rare foggy morning plant gives you the Sasquatch experience.

Bigfoot Outfits and Online Mysteries

In GTA Online, Rockstar has kept the legend alive with collectible clues and special outfits.
