What Are Contact Missions?

Contact missions are jobs assigned by notable in-game characters like Lamar, Gerald, and Trevor. You receive these missions as text messages on your in-game phone, and they can be played solo or with friends, making them perfect for new players seeking a risk-free introduction to cooperative gameplay.
