What Are CBG Gummies?

CBG (cannabigerol) is a non-psychoactive cannabinoid found in the hemp plant, often referred to as the "mother of all cannabinoids" because other cannabinoids are derived from its acidic form during plant development. CBG gummies are chewable edibles infused with CBG extract, making them a convenient and tasty way to consume this compound. Unlike THC, CBG will not get you high, and products are typically formulated to avoid psychoactive effects entirely.[1]
