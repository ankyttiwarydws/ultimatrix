What Are Cloud Kitchens?

Cloud kitchens, also known as ghost kitchens or dark kitchens, are commercial facilities built solely to prepare food for delivery. Unlike traditional restaurants, they have no dine-in space, allowing brands to focus on speed, consistency, and scaling up quickly. This model is a game-changer in urban India, where real estate is expensive and consumer demand for quick, quality meals is rising.

Cloud kitchens empower food entrepreneurs to experiment with multiple brands and cuisines under one roof. They use data-driven insights to optimize menus, reduce waste, and streamline operationsâ€”all while reaching customers exclusively through delivery apps.
