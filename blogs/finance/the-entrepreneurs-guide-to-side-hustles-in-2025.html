How to Choose Your Side Hustle

Not every idea will suit every entrepreneur. Ask yourself:

  • What skills or resources do I already have?
  • How much time can I realistically dedicate each week?
  • Am I looking for active income, passive income, or a mix?
  • Do I want to work online, offline, or both?
  • What startup costs am I comfortable with?

Start small, test your idea, and scale up as you learn. Many successful side hustlers began with just a few hours a week and gradually expanded as demand grew.
