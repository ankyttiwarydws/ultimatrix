2. Make Biweekly Payments

Instead of paying your loan once a month, split your payment in half and pay every two weeks. This strategy results in one extra payment each year, helping you pay off your loans faster and save on interest.
Example: On a 10-year repayment plan, you could finish a year early simply by switching to biweekly payments.

If you have good credit, a stable job, and possibly multiple loans, refinancing can be a powerful tool. It replaces your existing loans with a single loan, ideally at a lower interest rate or shorter term. This can save thousands in interest and simplify your monthly payments.
